Frequently asked questions
Is payment secure?
Yes. All payments are processed securely by Razorpay, which is PCI DSS Level 1 certified — the highest level of payment security. TeardownAI never stores your card details.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your billing settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.
What's the difference between Student and Pro?
Student plan is designed for PM aspirants, university students, and boot camp learners with 15 teardowns/mo, core interactive metrics, RICE frameworks, and PDF export. Pro plan is for active PMs needing unlimited teardowns, full features, and support.
Do you offer a free trial for Pro?
The Starter plan is permanently free and gives you 5 teardowns a month to evaluate the full report quality. We don't offer a time-limited trial — the free tier is designed to show you exactly what you're getting before upgrading.
What payment methods does Razorpay support?
Razorpay supports UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), net banking (50+ banks), and EMI options. It works seamlessly for Indian users.
